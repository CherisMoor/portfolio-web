About My Favorite Restaurants

  1. Ray's Country Smokehouse-Grill Down-to-earth haunt offering old-school smoked barbecue & classic sides, plus a bar & jukebox.

    Address:219 S Broad St, Gastonia, NC 28052

  2. Lotus Bar & Eatery Modern takes on regionally diverse American dishes, plus a sushi bar, served in industrial-hip digs.

    Address:1941 Hoffman Rd Suite #1, Gastonia, NC 28054

  3. Amber Jack's Seafood Whimsical steak & seafood eatery with a nautical theme supplying American classics & cocktails.

    Address:4253 S New Hope Rd, Cramerton, NC 28032

  4. Frankie T's Pizzeria Straightforward counter serve offering pizzas, cheesesteak sandwiches & pastas.

    Address:2211 N New Hope Rd, Gastonia, NC 28054

  5. Jersey Mike's New Jersey-based counter-serve sandwich chain serving jumbo subs filled with cold cuts & toppings.

    Address:3754 East Franklin Blvd Franklin Square, Gastonia, NC 28056

My Favorite Healthy Snack Choices

Some top choices of appetizers, desserts, and drinks I decide to get when I'm trying to make healthier choices when eating out

The two most healthy snacks or appetizers that I like are apple parfaits (a delicious yogurt cup filled with layers of apples, honey, and oats)

and Hummus (a chunky dip with avacado, tomato, and onions; usually eaten with whole grain chips).

Favorite Healthy Drink Choices

The two most popular drinks that I like to drink are Vitamin Water (a blend of water, fruit juices, and key vitamins; such as B,C, and D)

and Gatorade (a naturally flavored sports drink with electrolytes and vitamins).
